FAQ

What is KFC Roblox?

KFC Roblox is a restaurant simulator on Roblox that brings you the thrill of working in a bustling eatery with a diverse menu of wings and sides! We are not affiliated with the official KFC.

Is it easy to work with us?

Yes, if you learn our guides and follow the rules and regulations. Its simple to work with us.

Do I need to fill out a appliction to join?

Yes, to apply you need to be in the KFC roblox group and join the application center. Your results are published right after you finish the exam.
